📋 About the Career Development Plan (CDP)

The CDP commits to an outcome — workers progressing into higher-graded roles — not a fixed monthly cost. Ongoing salary obligations arise gradually as workers progress, so during the grant window your financial picture is largely your implementation cost.

Important notice — This tool provides indicative estimates only. The actual CTC Grant is subject to formal assessment, programme eligibility, and prevailing policies. Grant disbursement occurs after project completion. All figures in Singapore Dollars (SGD).
